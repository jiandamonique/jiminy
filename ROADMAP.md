# Jiminy — roadmap

This is a living document. Things will change.

---

## now
- Single HTML file, runs anywhere
- Live clock and greeting
- Private motivational phrase
- Countdown timer with sound, silent, and vibrate options
- Four themes
- Pause screen for privacy

---

## next
- AI helper layer (free tier + bring your own key)
- Incident log
- Logo and name finalized

---

## later
- Lightweight utility widgets (weather, task list, quote of day)
- Hosted personal version
- Cross-device sync

---

## always
- Free tier exists
- No ads
- No data sent anywhere without you
- One file, downloadable, works offline
